Tips for Best Results

Use Good Lighting

Take your photo in bright, natural light. Face a window or use a ring light for even illumination.

Face the Camera

Look directly at the camera with a neutral expression. Keep your face centered and visible.

Clear Background

Use a plain, light-colored background. Avoid busy patterns or dark colors.

Dress Appropriately

Wear contrasting colors to the background. Avoid white shirts against white backgrounds.

Hair Guidelines

Keep hair away from your face. Avoid heavy makeup or accessories that cover your features.

Correct Distance

Position camera at eye level, about 4-6 feet away. Ensure your full head and shoulders are visible.
